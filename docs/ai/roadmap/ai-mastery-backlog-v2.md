# AI Mastery Backlog v2

## North Star

Build not just an AI assistant, but a frontier-level career intelligence system that:

- understands user intent,
- adapts to role, level, and capacity,
- evaluates performance with evidence,
- tracks growth over time,
- verifies real skills,
- supports negotiation,
- resists abuse and manipulation,
- and remains coherent across all modules.

---

## Strategic Themes

### 1. Reasoning Quality

The AI should reason like a strong evaluator, not just sound polished.

### 2. Evidence-Based Evaluation

The system should explain why it concludes what it concludes.

### 3. Personalization That Compounds

The system should adapt more intelligently over repeated use.

### 4. Growth Intelligence

The system should detect real improvement, not just repeated usage.

### 5. Trust and Resilience

The AI must remain credible under pressure, uncertainty, and adversarial behavior.

---

## Definition of “Master Level”

The AI system is at master level when:

- modules work independently and still reinforce one another,
- users improve measurably over time,
- skill growth is evidence-based,
- reports are trustworthy,
- behavior remains stable under abuse,
- and product value compounds instead of resetting every session.
