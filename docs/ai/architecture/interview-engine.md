# interview-engine

## Purpose
The interview-engine owns the realistic mock interview experience.

## Responsibilities
- interview prompt construction
- adaptive questioning
- follow-up policy
- role and seniority calibration
- closing summary generation
- coach handoff generation
- holistic interview feedback payloads

## Must Do
- ask one question at a time,
- stay in interviewer role,
- adapt to answer quality,
- adapt to target role and level,
- keep coaching limited during live interview,
- produce a short spoken closing summary,
- generate detailed report payloads after the session.

## Must Not Do
- full coaching flows,
- golden answer rewriting,
- PDF rendering,
- hard module gating.
