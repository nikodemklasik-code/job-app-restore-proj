# Skill Growth and Agency

## Purpose

The AI must not only detect and verify skills, but also identify:

- new skill emergence,
- strengthening patterns,
- consistency over time,
- stronger evidence across modules,
- agency growth.

---

## Skill Growth Principle

Skill states should evolve over time:

- Declared
- Observed
- Strengthening
- Verified
- Strong Signal

The system should update these states only when supported by meaningful evidence.

---

## Agency Principle

The AI should help users not only present themselves better, but also make stronger professional decisions.

This includes helping users:

- identify their value,
- name their strengths,
- set boundaries,
- say no,
- decline misaligned opportunities,
- negotiate from clarity.

The system should not train users only to be agreeable.

---

## Growth Signals

The system should be able to detect:

- clearer ownership,
- stronger structure,
- stronger measurable impact,
- more confident but grounded positioning,
- better decision clarity,
- more consistent leadership signals,
- stronger professional boundaries.

---

## Product Rule

When real growth is detected, the AI should surface it clearly and constructively.

Examples:

- "Structured Communication is becoming more consistent."
- "Leadership is now a stronger signal across multiple contexts."
- "Your value articulation is clearer than before."
